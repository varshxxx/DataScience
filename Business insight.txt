1. Customer Concentration
Most customers hail from Asia or South America. 
Market more here and explore growth in underrepresented areas.

2. Signup Patterns
Identify peak years and seasons. 
Optimize marketing during high-signup periods, and offer promotions in quieter months.

3. Regional Nuances
Each region has unique customer traits. 
Tailor marketing and products to fit regional preferences.

4. Customer Retention
Compare new versus loyal customers. 
Develop loyalty programs and onboarding campaigns to enhance retention.

5. High-Value Targets
Spot trends among high-value customers.
 Prioritize regions yielding high-value customers and predict potential ones.
